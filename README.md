SQL_Myntra_Sales_Analysis

The objective of this project is to analyze the sales performance of Myntra using transactional data.
The goal is to identify top-performing products, revenue trends across months, and customer purchase behavior. 
This will help the business understand which product categories generate the most revenue, how sales vary over time, 
and what factors influence repeat customer purchases to improve business strategy and marketing decisions.

Database and Tools

•	MySQL
•	MySQLWorkbench 8.0 CE
